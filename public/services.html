Services

Copper & Optic Terminations Ltd specialise in sub contract Electronic wiring, cable and fibre optic assembly and test for the military, defence, aerospace, medical and professional electronics industries.

We aim to provide a world clss customer service by offering our customers on time deliveries of products to the highest possible quality standards, in-line with our BS EN ISO9001:2000 accreditation.

Copper & Optic will take your cable assembly or cable harness designs at whatever the stage of development and convert them into high quality products that will compliment your finished equipment, vehicle, aircraft or any other applications you manufacture.

All Copper & Optics cable and harness assembly are inspected by WHMA/IPC-A-620 certified inspectors. Our unrivalled experience in the field of electric assembly is long-standing through many years of working within an aerospace, defence and professional electronics environment.

From rack, panel, cubicle and cabinet wiring to the manufacturer of small enclosures having miniature PCBs, Copper & Optic will source standard or bespoke boxes, brackets and panels that may be plated or painted to your specifications and integrate wiring, printed circuit assembly and fibre optic termination before testing your functional Production Acceptance Test (PAT) or simple test for continuity and insulation resistance.

Copper & Optic are proud to employ staff that hold certification for soldering standard to IPC-J-STD-001 and acceptance standard IPC-A-610 PCB for printed circuit board assembly. This has enhanced our already high reputation for first class quality assembly and inspection processes. We can undertake small / low volume hand assembly of printed circuit boards of both through plated hole and surface mount technologies. Higher volume or complex printed circuit board assemblies are subcontracted to our approved suppliers. Our team of quality engineers will always perform an intricate final inspection of these assemblies here at Copper & Optic Ltd.

Here at Copper and Optic we have an in house facility to provide training to Cable Assembly acceptance stndard WHMA/IPC-A-620 for our own employees and also for your staff if required.

Your assembly operators or inspectors can be guided through the training by our industry experts in this highly recognised business standard. This acceptance standard is already a pre-requisite for selection to tender for many larger companies; this is a must have qualification in the field of electronics.

Here at Copper & Optic we have invested heavily into a range of automatic test equipment, as we strive to constantly improve our services. Our philosophy is ‘Get it right, not just first time, but every time’ and with this principle we test everything that we practically can, even when there may not be a specific requirement to test. We can use either DIT-MCO 2115 Circuit Analysers, CIRRIS or DYNALAB ATE (Automatic Test Equipment).

We can test up to 9000 test points enabling us to test backplanes, cubicles or control panels as well as larger wiring harnesses / looms. This facility is also offered as a stand-alone service, so that if you have a product that just requires testing but you don’t want to pay out tens of thousands of pounds for equipment, Copper & Optic can do it for you. Let us have the wiring / schematics and your test parameters and we will give you an accurate quote for test lead manufacture, programming and testing.

With our team of senior management having worked for some of the biggest names in the defence electronics industry as project engineering and sales management, Copper & Optic understand the importance of planning and executing a successful project. From the initial preparation of quotations, Copper & Optic endeavour to identify as many pitfalls as possible so that the resulting contracts run smoothly and are always delivered on time. We have our own fully developed integrated computer database with systems and reporting methods to inform the customer of progress throughout the project life.

Inside many assemblies, encapsulations or potting of components, batteries, printed circuit boards etc is common. Using epoxy’s, elastomers or other compounds, Copper and Optic can handle the design and provision of potting / mould and tooling, and commisioning acceptance before production is undertaken. life.
